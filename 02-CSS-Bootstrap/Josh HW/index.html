The Quick Rundown

My name is Josh Benjamin, and I am ecstatic to be here learning this valuable, challenging material. I hope we can all be of mutual help to one another as we go through this 6-month bootcamp journey together!

I am presently a professor at Drexel and the University of Pennsylvania. In both universities, I am in the English department, having obtained my undergrad degree in Advanced Linguistics and my Master's in TESL (essentially, teaching English to non-native English speakers).

On the side, I occassionally bartend and play poker competitively. Additionally, one of my hobbies--graffiti art--has become another way for me to earn income, as I use it to do commissioned artwork in a variety of locations.

I wish all the best to my current classmates/future coders as we get into this! Let's do this.
